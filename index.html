About Us

We are a collective of environmental advocates, concerned citizens, and local leaders dedicated to protecting the natural heritage and sustainable future of Governor Generoso, Davao Oriental.

Our Advocacy

Governor Generoso is home to breathtaking landscapes, rich marine biodiversity, and the globally recognized Mt. Hamiguitan Range Wildlife Sanctuary, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. However, a recently approved municipal ordinance promoting mining activities threatens these invaluable natural resources.

Our Mission

Raising awareness about the risks of mining in ecologically sensitive areas.

Encouraging local leaders to adopt sustainable economic strategies.

Advocating for policies that protect livelihoods, biodiversity, and natural resources.
